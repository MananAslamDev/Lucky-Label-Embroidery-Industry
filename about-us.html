Our Story

Since our establishment in 1997, we have proudly played a pivotal role in shaping Pakistan’s textile industry. As one of the few pioneering manufacturers of woven labels, we were among the first to introduce advanced technology and innovative techniques, setting new industry standards. Founded by Mr. Amir Jamil (late) and Mr. Irfan Jamil, our company was built on a foundation of hard work, integrity, and a relentless commitment to excellence.

Over the years, we have expanded our offerings to include a wide range of essential textile accessories, such as hang tags, printed labels, and polybags, providing our clients with comprehensive solutions that help elevate their brands and products. Our continued investment in state-of-the-art machinery and technology ensures that we deliver fast, reliable, and exceptional products, tailored to meet the unique needs of each client. As we move forward, our dedication to quality and service remains unwavering, and above all, customer satisfaction remains at the heart of everything we do.
